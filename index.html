OUR SYSTEM OF OPERATION

To be able to respond quickly to enquiries for placement of manpower to mobilize them at shortest possible time without any flaw, we put in place a system of operation which ensures highest quality service at the least of expenses. Our system of operation is depicted diagramically below:

1

CATEGORISATION OF WORKERS

We categorized our manpower resources under different heads such as skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled manpower. We also categorise them sectorwise.

2

I.Q. AND PSYCHOLOGICAL TESTS

We conduct an Intelligence and Psychological test to ensure that recruit-ed manpower match the recruitment of the client.

3

SCRUTINY AND SECURITY CHECK

Through a highly developed system, we scrutinize the selected persons' qualification, character and security background.

4

MEDICAL AND HEALTH EXAMINATION

To ensure that the selected person is fit for the job he has been chosen, we carry out a rigorous medical and physical fitness examination.

5

ORIENTATION/TRAINING PROGRAMME

Al-Dushary Enterprise has a well co-ordinated orientation/training programme to train and prepare the selected person for the job and the environment of the host country.

6

DESPATCH OF MANPOWER

To ensure smooth and timely despatch of the work force, we maintain an excellent working relation with different airlines operation between Dhaka and the labour importing countries.

7

MONITORING

Our work does not cease with the despatch of the workers. We try to monitor their performance so the we can act promptly to rectify any flaw in the operating channel. It may be mentioned here that we have our overseas offices to perform this task.

8

CONCLUSION

Manpower plays a vital role in the development of any organisation. We are sensitive towards that fact. Based on that understanding, we have organised the highest professional team to respond professionally to the need of our valued clients. If you are in deed of capable, efficient, well experienced and productive work force for any sector of economy, Al-Dushary Enterprise is only a mail- distance away.
